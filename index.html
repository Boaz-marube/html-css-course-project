FAQs

Find answers to your questions about PLANTFUEL products, nutrition, and delivery options.

What is protein content?

Our protein powders contain 20 grams of plant-based protein per serving. They are made from high-quality sources like pea protein and brown rice. This ensures you get the essential amino acids needed for muscle recovery.

Are there dietary restrictions?

Our products are 100% plant-based and free from common allergens like dairy and gluten. They are suitable for vegans and vegetarians. Always check individual product labels for specific ingredient information.

How does delivery work?

We offer fast shipping to ensure you receive your products promptly. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Can I return products?

Yes, we have a 30-day return policy for unopened products. If you're not satisfied, please contact our support team for assistance. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Where are ingredients sourced?

We source our ingredients from trusted suppliers committed to sustainable practices. Our lentils and quinoa are grown in eco-friendly environments. This ensures quality and supports responsible farming.
