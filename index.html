FAQs

Find answers to your questions about PLANTFUEL products, nutrition, and delivery options.

How can I join the early access list?

To join, simply click the ‘Early Access’ button on our homepage or in the navigation bar. It’ll take you straight to our signup form where you can enter your email and get first dibs on PlantFuel product drops, updates, and exclusive offers.

Are there dietary restrictions?

Our products are 100% plant-based and free from common allergens like dairy and gluten. They are suitable for vegans and vegetarians. Always check individual product labels for specific ingredient information.

How does delivery work?

We offer fast shipping to ensure you receive your products promptly. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Can I return products?

Yes, we have a 30-day return policy for unopened products. If you're not satisfied, please contact our support team for assistance. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Where are ingredients sourced?

We source our ingredients from trusted suppliers committed to sustainable practices. Our lentils and quinoa are grown in eco-friendly environments. This ensures quality and supports responsible farming.

ABOUT PLANTFUEL

PlantFuel is a new generation of plant-based, high-protein snacks designed for people who move. Created by certified nutritionist Tasha Muriuki, our mission is to fuel active, health-conscious lifestyles with clean, sustainable, and great-tasting snacks made from real, wholesome ingredients like lentils, quinoa, and natural flavorings.

We believe healthy eating should be simple, natural, and eco-friendly. That's why every PlantFuel product is free from additives, preservatives, and artificial sweeteners — giving you pure plant-powered energy you can feel good about.

Whether you're hitting the gym, hiking the trails, or powering through your workday, PlantFuel keeps you fueled, focused, and feeling fresh. Join our growing community of plant-fueled go-getters and experience the clean energy difference.
